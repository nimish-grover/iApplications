GIS Based INRM Planning Under MGNREGA
An online course for the technical staff of MGNREGA and Line Departments.
JOIN THIS COURSE
Students
6721 (Registered)
Review
(8 reviews)

What is this course?

  • Learn about Integrated Natural Resource Management (INRM)
  • Learn about Web based GIS application such as Bhuvan, Google Earth Pro etc.
  • Guidelines for developing GP Plans for INRM, Mission Water Conservation (MWC), Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojna (PMKSY) etc
    1. INRM
    2. GIS
    3. PMKSY
    4. MWC
    5. MGNREGA

Why this course?

  • To enhance knowledge in watershed, Command area development, Afforestation, Flood and Drought Management, Agriculture/Horticulture and allied livelihoods
  • To get hands-on practice of using Bhuvan Portal of NRSC and Google Earth Pro tools
  • Learn About GIS tools for thematic outputs
  • To learn about development of Gram Panchayat INRM plans under MGNREGA, PMKSY, MWC etc.

Who should do this course?

  • At block level: Barefoot technicians, technical assistants, assistant engineers
  • At District and State Level: All technical officers of Rural Development and line departments
  • All technical officers of Non Governmental Organisations working in the field of Natural Resource Management
Course Content
Total learning: 24 lessons / 5 quizzes
Time:3 Hours
